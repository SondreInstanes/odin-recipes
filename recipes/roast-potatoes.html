Oven Roasted Potatoes

Description

Delicious, extra crispy potatoes! It's easy, requires very little active cooking time, and uses mostly household ingredients!

Recipe derived from Kenji Lopez-Alt of Serious Eats.

Ingredients

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • Optional: Herbs! I recommend sage and thyme.

Steps

  1. Peel and chop potatoes into roughy equal pieces. The smaller the pieces, the lower the cooking time
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Add a few tablespoons of salt and a teaspoon of baking soda
  4. Parboil potatoes for 10-15 minutes. If a knife pierces the potato with little to no resistance they're ready
  5. While potatoes are boiling, mince your garlic (using a garlic crusher is fine)
  6. Add extra virgin olive oil to a pan, set the pan to medium heat, and add the garlic
  7. Add any herbs you wish to use. You could also add some chili flakes for some heat
  8. Cook until the garlic is just starting to turn golden brown, then strain the oil into a bowl (keep the garlic and herbs, we'll use them again later)
  9. Add the potatoes to the bowl of oil and mix! You'll want a mashed-potato like consistency on the outside
  10. Put the potatoes on a rack and add them to an oven that's been preheated to about 200c
  11. Cook for 30-40 minutes, turning halfway through (actual cooking time depends on your oven and the size of the potatoes)
  12. Toss the potatoes with the garlic (and herbs) immediately after completion. You could optionally add fresh herbs too!
  13. Enjoy!
